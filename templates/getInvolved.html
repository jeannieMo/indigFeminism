Who We Are
Why is it important to us?:
The Indigenous women of Mexico inspire us to create a resource that not only educates but also fosters an understanding and appreciation for their diverse cultures and traditions. Through our platform, we aim to provide a space where people can learn about Indigenous women's deeply impactful practices and gain insight into the systemic challenges they have faced and continue to confront.
Our mission is rooted in the belief that it is imperative to amplify and protect the voices of Indigenous women. By elevating their narratives, experiences, and struggles, we strive to challenge dominant narratives and dismantle systems of oppression that have historically silenced and marginalized Indigenous women.
We are dedicated to shedding light on the strength, resilience, and cultural contributions of Indigenous women in Mexico.
